Securing Data Plane in SDN
For this short talk, I would like to share my research on how to secure SDN on data plane. SDN is a new network structure that has a lot of advantages. But with new technology comes new threat. There are many security issues in SDN structure. In this talk, I'll first introduce SDN, including its advantages and disadvantages. Then, I'll explain why it can deal with some problems that tradition network can't handle, i.e. detecting a compromised switch automatically. Finally, I'll explain our solution and show some preliminary results of our experiment.
